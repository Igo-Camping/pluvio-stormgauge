How to copy this signature into Outlook

  1. Wait a moment for the logo image below to fully load.
  2. Click just before "Mark O’Callaghan", then hold Shift and click just after the "Atmos" line so the entire block (logo + text) is selected.
  3. Press Ctrl + C.
  4. Open Outlook → File → Options → Mail → Signatures, create or edit a signature, click into the editor, and press Ctrl + V.
  5. Save. Send a test email to yourself to confirm the logo renders.
